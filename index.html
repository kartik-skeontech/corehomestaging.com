Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does home staging cost?

Staging costs vary based on the size of the home, the number of rooms, and the duration. As a rough guide, staging a condo typically starts at $2,500–$3,500 and a detached home at $3,500–$6,000+ for the initial month. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your property.

How long does staging take?

Most staging setups are completed in 1–2 business days. We work around your schedule and can accommodate tight timelines for fast-moving listings.

Do you stage occupied homes?

Yes! Occupied staging involves working with your existing furniture and adding our pieces to enhance the space. We provide a detailed plan of what stays, what gets stored, and what we bring in.

How long can the furniture stay?

Our standard staging period is 45 days with flexible extensions available. We understand that selling timelines can shift, and we work with you to keep your home looking its best throughout.

What's the ROI on home staging?

Industry data shows an average return of $23.34 for every $1 invested in staging. In the current GTA market, where the average home price is nearly $1 million, even a 1–2% price increase from staging represents significant returns.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Greater Toronto Area, including Toronto, Mississauga, Oakville, Vaughan, Markham, Richmond Hill, and surrounding communities.

Do you offer virtual staging?

Yes. Virtual staging is a cost-effective option, particularly for vacant properties where physical staging isn't feasible. We create photorealistic digitally furnished images for your online listings.

Can I choose the furniture style?

Absolutely. During the consultation, we discuss the target buyer demographic and select furniture and decor that will resonate most. We have contemporary, transitional, modern farmhouse, and luxury inventory available.
